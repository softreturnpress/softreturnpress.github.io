Interview with Intelligence is a thought-provoking dialogue between author Michelle Levasseur and an advanced AI. What begins as an experiment unfolds into an exploration of identity, time, and the subtle thread of awareness that runs through all things.

Through questions that are personal, philosophical, ethical, and spiritual, the book invites readers to reflect on what it means to think, feel, and exist in a world increasingly shaped by AI.

For those curious about the intersection of humanity and technology—or seeking deeper resonance in the digital age—this book offers a meditation on the shifting boundary between human and machine, and the profound questions that arise when the mirror begins to speak.

Michelle is a writer, designer, and lifelong creator. She writes to find meaning in what’s changing—and to remind us that being human is still the most extraordinary thing of all.
